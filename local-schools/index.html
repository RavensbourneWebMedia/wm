Web Media BA

Future-proof students with the skills to become professional Web-makers in a connected world

Web Media?

The Web plays a huge part in our daily lives and underpins all our digital communications.

People everywhere are realising this and learning to create on the Web. Even in primary schools.

At Web Media we teach people-centred design, smart technologies and effective communication skills.

Our students become confident communicators, ambitious makers and creative coders, unafraid to use technology to bring their ideas to life.

What will you learn?

You don’t need prior knowledge of coding or design to join this 3-year BA. Web Media students are curious and open-minded, ready to play and experiment with communication and technology.

You will design and code Web products for real people, for instance you will work for a month in a primary school to create Web apps with kids and teachers.

You will strategise and produce social media campaigns, for instance you will work with the Royal Shakespeare Company to live-stream plays and manage thousands of followers.

Who will you become?

Web Media prepares you for jobs that don’t yet exist.

Most of our students start working in the industry while still studying.

At Ravensbourne we have a dedicated jobs agency, and our employability specialists help you applying for jobs within the creative sector, and even conduct mock interviews.

Our graduates start their careers as Web designers, app developers, UX designers, digital project managers, UI designers, social media producers and game designers among others. They work both within agencies and as freelancers.
